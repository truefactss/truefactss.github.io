Principles of Riches

Okonkwo Goodness Pelumi

PRINCIPLES OF RICHES.

Early this week, I was watching a movie. In the course of watching the movie, a girl in that movie said, “ I'm never going to go broke”. Meanwhile, all she does was eat and sleep. Now, this got me thinking-

HOW DO YOU INTEND TO GET RICH WHEN YOU DON'T WORK?

Sounds ridiculous right? Yes, I know. Nobody would ever want to affirm negativity in their life. Everybody wants the riches and silver of the world, but, how do you intend to execute that when you don't work towards it. “Everything you wish for is a script in your head. You have to act to be able to bring the script to life”- Nessa. Great! Now, the essence of this quote was to put you in an imaginative world, where all you wish for is like a script and you are the actor- you are only required to act to make the script a reality. Simple. In this illustration, your script stands for your dreams. So, you have to act your script to reality through hard work. -HEY! Have you ever asked yourself how you sleep knowing fully well that you are broke? If you ask me, I would say horrible. Knowing that you are broke gives you terrible nightmares. Except you are comfortable with being broke, which sounds almost impossible. More reason it is important to fix yourself up. In the next subheading, I would be discussing steps on how to lead you through the valley of brokeness to a brighter side.

4 STEPS TO LEAD YOU OUT OF THE VALLEY OF BROKENESS.

FIND SOMETHING TO DO: You can only work hard when you have a job. So, the first thing is to get yourself fixed up. Find something you are good at, and establish yourself. BE DETERMINED: Determination comes from the mind. This is what would serve as your energy to keep you going and focused when facing obstacles. WORK HARD: Hard work they say pays. Truly, it does when you are diligent with your work. AVOID ILLEGAL DOINGS: ill-gotten wealth from illegal deals, doesn't end well. Try as much as possible to avoid illegal dealing. Like I have always said, hard work is the only ticket to richness. I hope this helps you. I wish you luck.
