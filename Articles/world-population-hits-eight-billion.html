World Population hits 8 BILLION...

Amusan Abraham

World population hits eight billion on 15, November 2022.

As at Tuesday, the world population has risen to over eight billion according to the research done by the United Nation Population funds. The United Nation Population funds (UNFPA) says it's a milestone for human longevity and a way of signalling major improvement in public health.

The UNFPA

also stated that this milestone also came with warnings of worsening economic inequality and environmental damage. Although fewer risks and increased life expectancy are to be lauded, the moment is also a clarion call to look beyond numbers and for the governments to share responsibility for protecting people and the planet. In an interview with a concerned mother, she explained why it would be to the world's advantage, if the increase in the world population can be curbed. When asked if this advancement would aid global child(Ren) support, she said, "Well the way I understand it,if it well organised it should be good. At least some children will have a chance at being educated".

When asked if it wasn't organized, she said,

"Not really,for example a case trended on the Internet like 3 weeks ago. This particular couple had 4 children and they all live in one room apartment,the wife got pregnant again and,gave birth to 5children at once.same thing happened at ogbomosho about 2 years ago. Those ones too already had 5 kids down, the wife also gave birth to 5 at once making 10 plus the parents 12". Giving her honest opinion about the increase in larger families, 'more than one wife or husband', Mrs. Taiwo Aliu said that it is better to minimize it by enlightening and sensitizing families on the need to have a well planned family. She ended by asking what the world leaders has in store to end poverty and lack of basic amenities, not only to children but the world at large.
